Vision for a New Bangladesh

The 12-Party Alliance's 31-Point Reform Agenda

Download PDF

We propose the formation of a 'Constitution Reform Commission' to review and amend the constitution to reflect the aspirations of the people, ensuring checks and balances and preventing any future dictatorship.

Reinstating the Non-Party Caretaker Government system to oversee national elections, ensuring a free, fair, and participatory democratic process.

Implementing a 'Planned Housing' policy that ensures urban development does not encroach upon agricultural lands. Focusing on affordable housing for low-income groups.

Complete separation of the judiciary from the executive branch. Appointment of judges through an independent commission to ensure impartiality.
